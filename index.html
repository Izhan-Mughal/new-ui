NFT stands for non-fungible token. What does non-fungible mean? This means that a unit is non-interchangeable so that it can be one of a kind, but can also be sold and traded to other owners. As a token these items, which can include pictures, videos, and audio can be bought and traded all across the block chain. These tokens can be stored in your wallet or sent to other wallets depending on what you want to do with them. An NFT like a Hold Dao is a piece of digital artwork that you can own, but also has many other utilities including Rugifying and AR.

  1. You need to have an Eth wallet since Hold Dao is on the Ethereum blockchain (ERC1155)
  2. Use various applications like Binance and Coinbase to create a wallet
  3. Connect the MetaMask extension Click This to your wallet
  4. Pre-mint your own “state of the art” Hold Dao once it is time to launch!
